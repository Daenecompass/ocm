Case Numbering

Pika cases are auto numbered at creation using an incrementing counter maintained by the system. Organizations occasionally need to restart the numbering sequence (usually at the beginning of a new year) in order to organize cases more efficiently. The form below will allow you to change the case number counter to fit your needs.

The next opened case number will be: %%[new_number]%%  (Example: %%[new_number_ex]%%)
The total number of cases opened this year is: %%[cases_ytd]%%


Note: The counter will always be one less than the next case number that is generated.
