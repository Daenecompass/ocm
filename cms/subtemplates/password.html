Password Strength

Password Strength is determined by the number of different types of characters that are used in the password. These can include: numbers, lower case letters, upper case letters, and symbols. The strongest passwords include all of the above categories and the weakest only contain one.

Light: 1-2 categories (Ex. secretpass, MyPass, 123PASS)
Moderate: 3 categories (Ex. s3cR3tP4ss, MyPass@home, 123_PASS)
Strong: All 4 categories (Ex. $3cR3tP@ss, MyP4ss@h0me, 123_PaSS)

Password Length

Your Pika administrator may choose to require a minimum length for passwords to ensure that passwords are harder for outsiders and others to guess.
